Users of EduComp

These users would use the system to file and track complaints, view the status of their complaints, and communicate with the relevant parties.

Administrator

As an administrator of the system, you would have the ability to manage and oversee the complaint management process.

Management

As a member of the management team, you would be responsible for overseeing the complaint management process and ensuring that it aligns with the institution's goals and objectives.

Faculty

As a faculty user of the system, you would be responsible for addressing complaints that are assigned to you.

Student

As a user with student role in the system, you would be able to use the system to file and track complaints.

Parents

As a parent of a student, you can file and also view the complaints that are relevant to your child.
